1. Show the available databases:
   show databases

2. Use the "students" database:
   use students

3. Drop the "students" database (CAUTION: This operation permanently deletes the database and its collections):
   db.dropDatabase()

4. Create the "students" database:
   use students

5. Show the collections in the "students" database:
   show collections

6. Create the "ninjas" collection:
   db.createCollection("ninjas")

7. Create the "dojos" collection:
   db.createCollection("dojos")

8. Drop the "ninjas" collection:
   db.ninjas.drop()

9. Drop the "dojos" collection:
   db.dojos.drop()
